About Us

With over 1500 members the Institute represents individuals from all credit disciplines, including trade credit, consumer credit, banking credit, credit information, credit insurance, debt administration and collections and insolvency practitioners to name just some. The Institute’s aim is to raise professional standards in credit management, to increase the awareness of its importance as a crucial management function that plays a vital role in marketing, cash flow and profitability, and to educate and inform its ever-growing membership base.

Founded in 1953, the Institute of Credit Management is the center of expertise in South Africa for matters relating to the profession of credit .The Institute of Credit Management (ICM) is furthermore, the custodian of Credit and has taken care of the Credit industry for 67 years. The ICM has been improving and maintaining the quality of Credit Management qualifications and reputation incorporating knowledge of financial and other management training. The ICM has also built up the credibility and reputation of those in these fields.

The ICM is a Quality Assurance Partner (QAP) of the Quality Council for Trades & Occupations (QCTO). The ICM are the accredited professional body registered with South African Qualifications Authority (SAQA) accrediting qualifying learners on the NLRD database. The ICM is the examining, assessing and certification body for FASSET’s credit qualification.
- Credit Management can be studied at any accredited site in South Africa.
- Learner assessments are assessed twice per annum.
