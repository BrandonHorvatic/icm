Services

The ICM is the Professional Body for Credit Management and is Responsible for National Assessments and Moderation of all Credit Assessments which are held twice per annum.

To award Professional designations to members who meet the criteria on application and as determined by the board from time to time.

The schedule of designations may be obtained from the ICM on application.

To promote, assist with the formulation of, or oppose any legislation, regulation, or any form of control negatively affecting the credit industry, the profession of credit management or Members of the Institute.

ICM National Assessments: With the Covid-19 Pandemic the ICM made the decision with the learners safety to introduce Digital National Assessments to ensure the safety and well-being of our Learners, the ICM Education Committee and ICM Board made the decision to introduce Digital National Assessments.
